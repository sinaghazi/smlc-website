Sina's Multidimensional Leadership Cube (SMLC) is a framework designed to analyze leadership across personal, organizational, and societal levels. By examining the interactions between these dimensions, SMLC offers insights into leadership dynamics in our interconnected world. This tool can help individuals, organizations, and policymakers better understand and address complex leadership challenges.
